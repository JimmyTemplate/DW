SK

Highlight: Sk has kept many old ceremonial ritual or commonly know as legend alive on form of card game playing such as using the old school supporting structure that's placed between the student's legs in which the school will also be the first and the last to leave the football match. Despite its low budget, the school still produce a variety of depicted card picture.
What's unique: As mentioned Sk will be the first and the last school to leave the football match and also SK has a depicted special code for the alumni in the ceremonial closing events.
